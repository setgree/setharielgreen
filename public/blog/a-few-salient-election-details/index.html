a few salient election details

Of the many, here are a few that stick out:

  1. Harris’s got zero votes in 2020 because she has no natural constituency and isn’t good at managing a campaign. Why would things have been different this time?

  2. Trump is what McCain wanted to be, a true maverick. Getting Tulsi Gabbard and RFK Jr. onboard – dominating the crank vote without alienating more mainstream Republicans – is a remarkable accomplishment.

  3. Democrats have bungled governance, over and over. It was eye opening to me that Gavin Newsom has apparently always been capable of making San Francisco a nice place to walk around, but only did so when a foreign dignitary was visiting. Who would trust them/us to lead the country?

  4. Trump is a dangerous, failing moron. I still agree with Megan McArdle that it’s very bad for him to have command of nuclear weapons. I can find a few things to agree with him about, or to part strongly from the democrats on, and still put this reason first by a long shot when I’m ranking candidates.

  5. Elon is the man of the moment. What he’s accomplished in the past 5-10 years, not to mention getting this one right and putting his money where his mouth is about it, beggars belief. It was insanely stupid of Democrats to alienate him.i

  6. We carry on.
