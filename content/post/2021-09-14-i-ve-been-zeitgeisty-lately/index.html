I:

  • was a zoomtown-er in Park City in October 2020;

  • left my job to pursue personal projects in April 2021

  • got an unsanctioned booster shot a few days ago.1

Most things I do don’t get featured in trends sections. I wonder if the trend of being trendy will continue? I also am prompted to think about much the ideas that feel original or counternormative to me are just generally ‘in the air.’

  1. This one I will explain. I got the J&J shot in April 2021. When I got back to NY in August from traveling, I was worried about breakout cases. The fact that I got one dose in the first place rather than two seems to have been an artifact of clinical trial decisions rather than the result of a cognizable scientific principle, and we have strong theoretical reasons to believe that boosters provide additional immunogenic benefits; contra one of the dumbest things I’ve yet read in a newspaper, I think we can indeed make generalizations between vaccines. So I got a second. I had no compunction about lying to do so. I also didn’t think it was morally relevant that other people who are more deserving of a dose haven’t gotten theirs yet, because the shot I took, on the margin, probably equals one fewer dose being thrown out.↩︎
