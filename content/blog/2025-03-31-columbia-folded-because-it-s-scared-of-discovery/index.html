In the news:

White House Cancels $400 Million in Grants and Contracts to Columbia

Columbia Agrees to Trump’s Demands After Federal Funds Are Stripped

Columbia President Is Replaced as Trump Threatens University’s Funding

At my local cafe last week, folks seemed incensed by this. Why didn’t Columbia have more courage, they asked? Why couldn’t the Ivy League band together and mount a hearty legal defense?

I think @patio11 has the right answer here:

I’d take essentially any odds on a wager that at some point in the 10-15 years, some high-level administrators wrote, in their work emails, things that rhyme with: “let’s hire this person because they’re [not] [X protected group]”. I don’t think Columbia is scared of Students for Fair Admissions. I do think they’re scared of the DOJ. I’d bet that there are at least a few folks there who are itching to initiate FBI raids in a “how do you like it?” spirit. I don’t think they’d be looking at jail time, but honestly, who knows?
